# Game Design Document (GDD)

## Game Title
Gods of the Fallen Realms (Working Title)

## High Concept
A next-generation MMORPG action RPG blending the cinematic combat of God of War with the open-world depth and mystery of Elden Ring. Players explore a vast, interconnected world, battling gods, monsters, and each other in a persistent online universe.

## Genre
Action RPG / MMORPG

## Platform
PC (Windows), with future plans for consoles

## Target Audience
Mature gamers who enjoy challenging combat, deep lore, and online multiplayer experiences.

## Core Gameplay
- **Combat:** Skill-based, weighty melee and ranged combat with combos, parries, dodges, and special abilities.
- **Exploration:** Open world with secrets, dungeons, vertical traversal, and environmental storytelling.
- **Progression:** Leveling, stats, gear, skills, and unique character builds.
- **Multiplayer:** Persistent world, PvE and PvP, guilds, trading, and world events.
- **Boss Fights:** Multi-phase, cinematic encounters with unique mechanics.

## Story & Setting
- **World:** A shattered realm where gods once ruled, now in chaos. Players uncover ancient secrets and shape the fate of the world.
- **Narrative:** Branching quests, environmental storytelling, and player-driven choices.

## Art & Audio
- **Visuals:** High-fidelity, dark fantasy art style with dynamic weather and day/night cycles.
- **Audio:** Epic orchestral soundtrack, immersive sound effects, and voice acting.

## Technical Overview
- **Engine:** Unreal Engine 5.4 (C++ & Blueprints)
- **Networking:** Dedicated server architecture
- **AI:** Advanced enemy and boss behaviors
- **Animation:** Motion-matching and procedural animation

## Monetization
- Buy-to-play with optional cosmetic microtransactions (no pay-to-win)

## Roadmap
1. Core combat and movement prototype
2. Open world and networking
3. RPG systems and progression
4. Bosses and dungeons
5. Multiplayer features
6. Polish and content expansion
