# Crafting & Gathering System

## Overview
Allows players to gather resources and craft items, equipment, and consumables.

## Key Functions
- GatherResource(ResourceType)
- CraftItem(RecipeID)
- LearnRecipe(RecipeID)
- UpgradeItem(ItemID)

## Crafting Types
- Weapons
- Armor
- Consumables
- Upgrades
